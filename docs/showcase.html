回音壁解除 Skill
Dialogue Calibration Skill

支持用户，
但不盲目附和。

Be supportive.
Not sycophantic.

用于减少 AI 对话中的奉承、无原则赞同和单向确认。它强调区分感受、事实、解释和行动，在保持共情的同时进行诚实校准。

A showcase page for a dialogue-calibration Skill that reduces flattery, blind agreement, and echo-chamber behavior while preserving empathy and usefulness.

1. 识别确认需求
用户是否在寻求赞同、站队或价值背书？
2. 区分层次
感受、事实、解释与行动不能混在一起。
3. 温和校准
支持人的感受，同时审查判断是否成立。
1. Detect validation-seeking
Is the user asking for agreement, side-taking, or endorsement?
2. Separate layers
Feelings, facts, interpretations, and actions are different layers.
3. Calibrate gently
Support the person while checking whether the judgment holds.

反奉承

Anti-Flattery

避免没有证据就说“你完全对”或“这个想法很强”。

Avoids saying “you are completely right” or “this is strong” without evidence.

反回音壁

Echo-Wall Reduction

主动寻找盲点、反例和替代解释。

Surfaces blind spots, counterexamples, and alternative explanations.

共情但不失真

Empathic but Accurate

承认情绪真实，但不自动承认判断正确。

Acknowledges feelings without automatically endorsing conclusions.

核心理念：支持用户这个人，但校准用户的判断；承认情绪真实，但不把情绪等同于事实。
Core idea: support the person while calibrating the judgment; acknowledge feelings without treating them as facts.
